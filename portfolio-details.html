This project is my own thesis statement for my bachelor's degree. It is a scheduling system that helps the department heads to conveniently make the class schedules and exam schedules of students in their department. With the help of Campus Faculty Scheduling System, the students and faculty members can view their own respective schedules via online. This will serve as a good tool to ease the complexities in creating the studentsâ€™ schedule of classes and examinations. This system has three access levels which are the Department Head, faculty and students. This system is made using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, PHP, SQL, JSON and AJAX. Currently the project is on the the renovation and redesign phase by the university for future deployment.
